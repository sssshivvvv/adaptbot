COT & ACTION SEQUENCE:

To play the music, I will first fetch the music_player. If the music_player is not already switched on, I will switch it on. Then, I will wait for a few seconds to ensure that the music player is ready to play. Finally, I will select a song and play it.

pick_up_rec("music_player", "updated_onto.ttl") #picking up the music player
switch("music_player", "updated_onto.ttl") #switching on the music player
wait(5) #waiting for 5 seconds
switch("music_player", "updated_onto.ttl") #switching off the music player
